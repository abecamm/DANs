Toolbox Flow (extraction day):

  1. Drag "Grab DAN Tokens" to your bookmarks bar
  2. In the DAN Extractor, click "Look Up" on a row → Toolbox opens
  3. Paste sequence number → click "Get Check"
  4. Click the "Grab DAN Tokens" bookmarklet → enter check #
  5. Back in DAN Extractor, click "Paste Tokens"

Email Flow (next day — on Google Sheets):

  1. Drag "DAN Email Gen" to your bookmarks bar
  2. In the DAN Google Sheet, click on column O of the row you want to email
  3. Click the "DAN Email Gen" bookmarklet
  4. First time: enter your name (remembered for the session)
  5. Review the generated message → click "Copy Message"
  6. Paste into Salesforce and send
